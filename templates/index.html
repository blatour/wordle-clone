How to Play

  • Guess the word in 10 tries or less
  • Each guess must be a valid word of the correct length:
    • Easy: 4 letters
    • Normal: 5 letters
    • Hard: 6 letters
  • After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word:
